# Approval Policy

The approval policy explains when employees need extra review before an expense
can be reimbursed.

## Manager Approval

- Any single expense above INR 5000 needs manager approval.
- Meal claims above the normal meal limit need manager approval and business
  justification.
- Taxi claims above INR 1500 per day need manager approval.
- Missing receipt claims need manager approval.
- Policy exception claims need manager approval before Finance review.

## Finance Approval

- Any single expense above INR 25000 needs Finance approval.
- Expenses above published exception limits are not reimbursed unless Finance
  grants a special exception.
- Finance can reject claims that are personal, duplicated, unsupported, or
  submitted outside the 30-day claim window.
