# Reimbursement Policy

The reimbursement policy defines how employees submit claims and what evidence
Finance needs before paying approved expenses.

## Submission Rules

- Employees should submit expense claims within 30 days of the expense date.
- Every claim must include amount, category, date, description, and business
  purpose.
- Claims above INR 500 require a receipt.
- Claims with missing receipts may need manager approval.
- Finance may reject duplicate claims, personal purchases, fines, penalties, and
  unsupported expenses.

## Payment Timeline

- Approved claims are normally reimbursed in the next payroll cycle.
- Claims with policy exceptions may take longer because they need manager or
  Finance review.
- Employees should keep original receipts until reimbursement is complete.
