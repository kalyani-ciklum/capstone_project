# Travel Expense Policy

The travel expense policy explains which travel costs employees may claim when
the travel has a clear business purpose and is approved by the employee's
manager or project lead.

## Covered Travel Costs

- Taxi and cab expenses are reimbursable for client meetings, airport transfers,
  late-night business travel, and travel between official work locations.
- Taxi claims up to INR 1500 per day are normally allowed when the employee
  submits a receipt and business reason.
- Taxi or cab claims above INR 1500 and up to INR 5000 need manager approval.
- Personal commute between home and regular office location is not reimbursable.
- Train and bus tickets are reimbursable for business travel with valid proof.
- Domestic flights should be booked in economy class unless a manager approves
  an exception.
- Hotel or lodging costs up to INR 5000 per night are reimbursable for approved
  business travel.

## Required Evidence

- Employees must submit receipts, travel dates, destination, business purpose,
  and client or project name.
- Missing receipts may delay reimbursement or require manager approval.
