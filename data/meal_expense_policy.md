# Meal Expense Policy

The meal expense policy covers food expenses during approved business work,
client meetings, official travel, and late-night project work.

## Meal Limits

- Breakfast is reimbursable up to INR 250 per person.
- Lunch is reimbursable up to INR 500 per person.
- Dinner is reimbursable up to INR 800 per person.
- A general meal claim without meal type should stay within INR 700 per person.
- Meal expenses above the normal limit and up to INR 1200 need manager approval
  and a written business justification.
- Meal expenses above INR 1200 are not reimbursable unless Finance grants a
  special exception.

## Non-Reimbursable Meal Items

- Alcohol is not reimbursable.
- Personal celebrations, family meals, and non-business food delivery are not
  reimbursable.
- Tips are reimbursable only when included in the official restaurant bill.

## Required Evidence

- A receipt is required for meal claims above INR 500.
- The claim should include date, attendees, business purpose, and project name.
